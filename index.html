Swiss Cobotics Competence Center (S3C)

Advancing collaborative robotics for Swiss industry

About S3C

The Swiss Cobotics Competence Center (S3C) is a national hub dedicated to supporting Swiss manufacturing companies in adopting collaborative robotics (“cobotics”) - where robots work safely and flexibly alongside humans to boost productivity and innovation.

Founded as a non-profit in 2022 and based at Switzerland Innovation Park Biel/Bienne, S3C brings together industry and research partners to create a vendor-neutral environment where companies can test, validate, train and implement cobotic solutions tailored to real production needs.

Mission

S3C’s mission is to:

  • Accelerate cobotic adoption in Swiss industry
  • Provide practical test & demonstration infrastructure
  • Bridge research and real-world manufacturing applications
  • Support SMEs with tailored consulting and training

By offering hands-on experimentation and expertise, S3C helps companies evaluate how collaborative robots can enhance flexibility, quality and competitiveness.

Core Capabilities

S3C provides:

  • Cobotic Base Cells (CBCs) - modular, industrial-like test environments to explore cobotic tasks safely and confidentially.
  • Application-specific demonstrations in precision, medtech, lab automation and machinery.
  • Training, evaluation, and integration support from experts.

Get Involved

Whether you represent a small or large enterprise, you can connect with S3C to explore cobotic adoption, join workshops, or arrange trials.
